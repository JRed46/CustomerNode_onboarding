Disclaimer

We seek to promote mental health wellness, not to treat diagnosed mental illness. It is well understood that physical exercise helps prevent physical disease. However, for someone with symptoms of heart disease, the proper course of action is to see a doctor, not self-prescribe jogging. Similarly, this application is intended for healthy individuals looking to maintain their mental health and prevent a more serious episode. The application may be useful for diagnosed individuals as a component of prescribed care but should not be used as a substitute for professional evaluation. If you are concerned about your wellbeing, please utilize MIT resources or see a private professional.
